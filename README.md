##  Features

###  Design & User Experience
- **Fully Responsive Layout** — Seamlessly adapts to all screen sizes from mobile to desktop
- **Dark / Light Mode Toggle** — User preference is saved in `localStorage`
- **Smooth Scrolling** — Polished navigation with anchor links and offset for fixed header
- **Fade‑In Animations** — Content reveals as you scroll for a polished feel
- **Scroll Progress Bar** — Visual indicator of reading progress at the top of the page
- **Back‑to‑Top Button** — Appears when you scroll down for quick navigation

###  Intelligent Support Chatbot
- **AI‑Powered Responses** — Understands natural language questions about products, pricing, support, and more
- **Quick‑Reply Buttons** — One‑click access to common questions
- **Human Escalation** — When the bot can't help, users can connect with a simulated human agent
- **Live Status Indicators** — Shows "Online", "Busy", or "Offline" in the chat header
- **Human Agent Badge** — Visible when a human is connected, building trust
- **Typing Indicators** — Realistic bot typing simulation
- **Auto‑Open** — Chat appears after a few seconds to engage visitors

###  Subscription & Payment Flow
- **Pricing Plans** — Three tiers: Starter ($29/mo), Professional ($79/mo), Enterprise ($199/mo)
- **Monthly / Yearly Toggle** — Yearly plans include a 20% discount
- **Payment Modal** — Collects card details with basic validation
- **Simulated Subscription** — Provides success feedback after "payment"

###  Team & Personalization
- **CEO Avatar Upload** — Click the CEO avatar to upload a custom photo, saved in `localStorage`
- **Team Member Profiles** — Displays key leadership with initials as placeholders

###  Content-Rich Sections
- **Hero** — Bold headline, stats, and visual capability grid
- **Services** — 8 product/service cards with icons and badges
- **Feature Tabs** — AI/Cloud/Data capabilities with interactive tab switching
- **About / Timeline** — Company story and key milestones
- **Testimonials** — Auto‑playing carousel with client feedback
- **FAQ Accordion** — Expandable answers to common questions
- **Blog** — 6 latest articles with read‑more links
- **Careers** — 4 job openings with "Apply Now" badges
- **Resources** — Whitepapers, demos, and case studies
- **Newsletter** — Email subscription form with validation
- **Partners** — Client / partner logo showcase
- **Trust Badges** — ISO 27001, GDPR, 24/7 support, 99.9% uptime
- **Contact** — Form with validation and contact info cards

###  Additional Features
- **Cookie Consent Banner** — GDPR‑compliant with Accept / Decline options
- **Analytics‑Ready** — Clean structure for adding Google Analytics or other tracking
